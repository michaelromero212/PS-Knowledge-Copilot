# Services Overview
> Source: GreenScape Employee Handbook

GreenScape Lawn & Landscape offers three main categories of service. Use this as a quick reference when a customer asks "what do you all do?"

## 1. Sprinkler / Irrigation
- New sprinkler system design and installation.
- Spring start-ups and backflow testing.
- Fall winterization (blow-outs) to prevent frozen, cracked pipes.
- Repairs: broken heads, leaks, valve and controller problems.

## 2. Lawn Care
- **Mowing**: weekly or bi-weekly cuts, including edging and blowing off walks and drives.
- **Fertilization & weed control**: a season-long program of scheduled treatments.
- **Seeding & aeration**: overseeding thin lawns and core aeration to relieve compaction.

## 3. Yard Maintenance
- Tree and shrub trimming and pruning.
- Brush and hedge trimming.
- Cleanup and haul-away of trimmings and yard debris.

## What we do NOT do
- Large tree removal or stump grinding (we refer these out to a partner arborist).
- Hardscaping (patios, retaining walls) and mulch delivery are seasonal add-ons, not year-round services.

## Service philosophy
Every customer gets a named crew lead and a satisfaction guarantee: if they are not happy with a visit, we return to make it right at no charge. When in doubt, offer to send the crew back rather than argue.
