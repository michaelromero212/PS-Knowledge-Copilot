# Scheduling, Billing & Policies
> Source: Office Handbook — Customer Service

## Service area
- We serve the metro area and suburbs within about a **25-mile radius** of the shop.
- Addresses outside that radius are handled case by case; check with the office manager before quoting.

## Scheduling
- Recurring services (mowing, fertilization) run on **fixed routes**, so customers keep the same day each week or cycle.
- One-time services (trimming, aeration, repairs) are booked in **half-day arrival windows** (morning or afternoon), not exact times.
- Customers get a **text reminder the day before** a scheduled visit.

## Weather and rescheduling
- Rain or unsafe conditions push work to the next available day; we notify customers by text.
- Customers can skip or reschedule a recurring visit with **at least 24 hours notice** at no charge.

## Billing
- Recurring services are **billed monthly** for that month's visits.
- The fertilization program can be paid **per treatment** or **prepaid for the season at a 10% discount**.
- We accept **credit/debit, ACH bank transfer, and check**. Cards on file are charged automatically after each billing cycle.
- Invoices are emailed; a customer can request paper billing.

## Cancellation
- No long-term contracts for standard services — customers can cancel recurring service anytime with **one week's notice**.
- Sprinkler installations require a signed estimate and a deposit; the deposit is refundable up until materials are ordered.

## Satisfaction guarantee
- If a customer is unhappy with a visit, we return within a few business days to make it right at **no charge**. Always offer the return visit first.
